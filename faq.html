Frequently Asked Questions

How much do you charge?

Our prices are based on the size of the lot and its contours and obstacles. We will come out and inspect the property free of charge before giving the price quote.

Do you offer contracts?

Yes. We offer seasonal contracts.

Do you offer discounts or promotions?

We offer a 10% discount for contract customers who require our services on a weekly basis. We offer one free lawn cut for any customer whose referral results in a signed contract. We offer a 10% discount for senior citizens.

What kind of payments do you accept?

We accept cash, personal or business checks.

What are your payment policies?

Our payment policies are flexible for customers with annual contracts. We will accept payment at the time services is completed. If preffered, we will bill you on a monthly basis. Payment is due upon receipt of the invoice.

Are you licensed and insured?

Yes. We are licensed and insured.
